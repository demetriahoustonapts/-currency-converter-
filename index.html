Understanding Exchange Rates

Exchange rates represent the value of one currency relative to another and fluctuate constantly based on global economic factors, market demand, geopolitical events, and central bank policies. The rates displayed on QuickCurrency.net are mid-market rates for informational purposes. When conducting actual currency exchange transactions through banks, credit cards, or money transfer services, you may encounter slightly different rates that include service fees or margins.
