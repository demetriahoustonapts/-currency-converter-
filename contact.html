Response time

We typically respond within 1–3 business days. During high volume periods, replies may take longer. If your request is urgent and related to a financial transaction, contact your bank, payment provider, or card issuer directly.

Important note about rates

QuickCurrency provides estimates for informational purposes only. Exchange rates change frequently, and banks, cards, and payment services may apply markups and fees. Always confirm the final rate and total with your provider before completing a purchase, transfer, or exchange.

Safety & privacy
